About

Zamzam
University

Zamzam University of Science and Technology (ZUST) is a multi-campus, non-profit institution recognized by Somalia's Ministry of Education, Culture, and Higher Education. Established to expand access to quality education, the university delivers programs that combine academic rigor with practical application. Through its faculties and research initiatives, ZUST prepares graduates to address real-world challenges and contribute to national development.
